About Papers Talks CV

I'm a post-doc advised by Mike Hicks within the programming languages group (PLUM) of the Computer Science department at the University of Maryland, College Park.

My interest center around the modeling, measuring, and limiting (adversary) knowledge in the context of information security. I'm also interested in probabilistic programming which serves as a convenient tool for these tasks, and secure multi-party computation which is abound in knowledge-related problems.

See more in my papers and my talks.
